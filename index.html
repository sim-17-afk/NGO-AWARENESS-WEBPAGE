Who We Are

InAmigos Foundation is a passionate youth-led NGO committed to driving positive societal transformation. Through collective action, strategic campaigns, and local community outreach, we focus on breaking the cycles of poverty, ignorance, and environmental neglect.

Follow our journey: #InAmigos #SocialGood #MakeAnImpact

Our Ongoing Initiatives

Project Shiksha

Providing free evening tuition classes, distribution of essential stationery, and basic digital literacy training for underprivileged children.

🌱 Impact: 500+ Children Educated

Eco-Drive Initiative

Focused on urban tree plantation drives, waste segregation awareness campaigns, and eliminating single-use plastics in local markets.

🌍 Impact: 2000+ Saplings Planted

Project Annapurna

A zero-hunger campaign dedicated to distributing healthy, freshly cooked meals to homeless individuals and daily wage laborers every weekend.

🍲 Impact: 10,000+ Meals Served
